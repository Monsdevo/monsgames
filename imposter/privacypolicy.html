When you use the App, we may collect certain information automatically from your device, including:

  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, and unique device identifiers.

Imposter includes a local multiplayer feature that allows users to play with others on the same Wi-Fi network.

Why we ask for permission: The App requests access to your local network solely to discover and connect with other devices for gameplay purposes.
