Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 06, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to Monsavor. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy will inform you about how we handle your data when you use our app.

Data Collection and Use

Information We Collect

  • User Account Information:
    • Email address and password (for email registration)
    • Username (optional, user-defined)
    • Apple ID information (when using Apple Sign In)
    • Guest account information (temporary email for guest users)
  • User-Provided Content:
    • Recipe preferences and dietary restrictions
    • Saved recipes and favorites
    • Custom recipe modifications
    • Shopping list items
    • Recipe ratings and likes
  • Automatically Collected Information:
    • Device information (model, operating system version)
    • App usage statistics and performance data
    • Authentication tokens and session data
    • Premium subscription status and purchase history
  • Recipe Generation Data:
    • Ingredients and cooking preferences you input
    • Generated recipe content
    • Recipe interaction data (likes, saves, shares)

Authentication and Account Management

Our app provides secure authentication services:

  • Email/password authentication
  • Apple Sign In integration
  • Guest account creation with temporary credentials
  • User profile management (username, email)
  • Session management and secure token storage

Premium Features and Purchases

We use Apple's StoreKit for in-app purchases:

  • Premium subscription management
  • Purchase verification and validation
  • Subscription status tracking
  • Receipt validation and storage
  • Premium feature access control

Data Storage and Processing

Cloud Storage:

  • User account information (email, username, premium status)
  • Saved recipes and favorites
  • User likes and interactions
  • Shopping list data
  • Authentication tokens and session data

Local Storage:

  • App preferences and settings
  • Generated recipes (temporary)
  • Offline recipe data
  • User interface preferences

Recipe Generation Processing:

  • Recipe generation requests are processed through our secure AI service
  • Input ingredients and preferences are processed to generate personalized recipes
  • Generated recipes are returned and may be stored locally or in cloud

Tracking and Advertising

Our app uses advertising services (Google AdMob) that may collect:

  • Device identifiers (IDFA)
  • Usage data and analytics
  • IP address for ad delivery
  • App interaction data for personalized ads

These services may track users across apps and websites as permitted by device settings and user consent.

Third-Party Services

Google AdMob

Our app uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and process data according to their privacy policy, including:

  • Device information
  • IP address
  • Advertising identifiers
  • Usage data

For more information about how Google uses your data, please visit: Google Privacy Policy

Apple Services

Our app uses Apple's services including:

  • StoreKit for in-app purchases
  • Apple Sign In for authentication
  • iCloud for data synchronization (if applicable)

Cloud Services

Our app uses secure cloud services for:

  • User authentication and account management
  • Cloud data storage and synchronization
  • Real-time data processing
  • User session management

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve our recipe generation service
  • To personalize your experience and recommendations
  • To manage your account and premium subscriptions
  • To analyze app performance and usage patterns
  • To maintain and improve app functionality
  • To process in-app purchases and subscription management
  • To provide customer support and respond to inquiries

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS)
  • Secure authentication tokens
  • Local data encryption where applicable
  • Regular security updates and monitoring

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data stored in our systems
  • Opt-out of data collection for advertising
  • Request information about how your data is used
  • Correct or update your account information

Children's Privacy

Our app is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed and stored in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for international data transfers.
