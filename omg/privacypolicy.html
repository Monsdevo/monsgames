Privacy Policy for OMG

Last updated: February 19, 2026

OMG ("the App") is a privacy-first application provided by Mons Games. We believe in keeping your data yours. This policy explains what information we collect and how it is used.

1. No Accounts & No Registration

OMG does not require you to create an account, log in, or provide any personal information (such as your name, email address, or phone number) to use the App. We do not have access to your identity.

2. Information We Collect

A. Local Usage Data (On-Device)
All your Watchlists, Favorites, and Preferences are stored locally on your device using Apple's secure storage system (Keychain and UserDefaults). This data never leaves your device and is deleted if you uninstall the App.

B. Remote Control Feature
The App includes a "Remote Control" feature to control Smart TVs (Samsung, LG, Vizio, etc.).

Local Network Access: To discover and connect to your TV, the App scans your local Wi-Fi network.
No Cloud Processing: All communication between the App and your TV happens directly over your local network. We do not collect or upload your network information or device details to any external server.

C. Third-Party Services
We use The Movie Database (TMDB) API to provide movie and TV show information, images, and metadata. By using the App, you acknowledge that content data is provided by TMDB. We do not share your personal data with TMDB.

3. Data Security

Since we do not collect personal data or store your usage history on our servers, there is no risk of your personal data being breached from our side. Your local data is protected by your device's security features (FaceID, Passcode).

4. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: monsgemas@gmail.com
